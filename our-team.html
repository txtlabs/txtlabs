Overview

TXT Labs is a consulting firm specializing in software development & IT services, focused on providing end-to-end solutions, including ideation, development, and marketing. Founded in 2016, TXT Labs provides clients a wide range of industry-leading products and services, utilizing our extensive expertise in the tech and educational sectors. The firm is comprised of a talented and experienced team of software engineers, designers, and marketing professionals, working alongside inner-city youth from one of our partnering non-profit organizations to meet and exceed our clientsâ€™ needs and expectations. The firm is headquartered in Los Angeles, with offices in San Francisco and New York.

Our Principles
PROVIDE INDUSTRY-LEADING INNOVATIVE SOLUTIONS
We provide outstanding service to businesses for web and mobile app development, IT consulting services, and Educational STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) consulting. Our team is committed to improving your business's efficiency and profitability through innovative technology.
Lifting community through transforming lives
We believe transforming young men of color is the catalyst of change in low-income communities. TXT Labs improves the lives of traditionally underrepresented youth in South L.A. by providing them with instruction and experience in design, software development, and entrepreneurship, while creating job opportunities.
Make a social impact
By working with us, you are providing 21st-century youth and workforce development opportunities and experience to at-risk youth in our most challenging communities. All profits go back into the community and fund Teens Exploring Technology and their Coding Leadership Academies.
